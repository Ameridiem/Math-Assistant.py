
- Enter an equation only in terms of x.
- If you want to multiply, use this character: "*"
- More complex equations involving variables in exponents, fractions,
logarithmic, exponential, and trig functions are not recognized by this
program as of date!
- Fractions and decimals are also not recognized by this program.
- Your equation should look something like this: x^2 +5x + 6x^4 - 2x - 29
- If writing equation in brackets to a power, then the stuff inside the brackets needs to be in order from highest power to lowest power.
